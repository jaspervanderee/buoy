    Quick verdict

    Best for: US spenders who want bitcoin rewards on daily purchases without running their own node.

    Consider if: You plan to fund the Fold Card with bitcoin or pay big bills via ACH to earn 0.25%-1.5% back.

    Not ideal when: You need worldwide access, true self-custody, or want to avoid KYC.

    Getting started

    1. Download Fold and verify your identity

    Time: ~10 minCost: FreeRisk: Low
    1. Install Fold from the App Store and start the Fold Card application
    2. Enter your legal name, SSN, and residential (non-PO Box) address for KYC
    3. Answer any identity questions and wait for the approval email (manual reviews can take 3-4 business days)

    Gotcha: Mistyped answers or PO Box addresses cause verification failures-double-check the details before resubmitting.

    2. Add dollars or bitcoin

    Time: ~5 minCost: Up to 1% (non Fold+)Risk: Medium
    1. Link your bank for ACH transfers or direct deposit if you plan to load USD regularly
    2. Use Push to Card to sell bitcoin inside Fold and instantly fund the card (Fold+ pays 0%, Basic pays 1%)
    3. Keep transfers within instant move limits ($1,500 per deposit, higher tiers unlock after six months of Fold+)

    Gotcha: Instant debit transfers and loads are capped; large amounts may need traditional ACH, which can take a few days.

    3. Start earning rewards

    Time: ~2 minCost: FreeRisk: Low
    1. Spin the Rewards Wheel every day for sats and buy gift cards through Fold to unlock extra cash back
    2. Use the Fold Visa card for signature purchases to earn 0.5% base and catch category or merchant boosts worth 1.5%-5%
    3. Pay recurring bills with ACH Bill Pay to earn up to 1.5% on the first $2,500 that month based on last month's qualified spend

    Gotcha: ACH rewards stop once you exceed last month's qualified spend, and boosted card purchases don't count toward next month's allowance.

    Quick answers

    Why did my ACH Bill Pay rewards drop to 0%?

    ACH rewards only apply up to the total qualified spend you logged the previous month; once current ACH payments exceed that dollar amount, the reward rate falls to 0% until the next cycle.

    Do boosted Visa transactions count toward qualified spend?

    Only Visa Signature transactions that earned the base 0.5% reward count. Purchases in boosted categories, PIN transactions, and prior ACH payments do not increase next month's allowance.

    What exactly happens when I push to card?

    Push to Card sells bitcoin for USD and loads those dollars to your Fold Card balance-you're not sending BTC directly onto the card, so the conversion can create a taxable event.

    Key terms
    Qualified spend
    Your prior month's bitcoin buys, gift cards bought with Fold balance (counted 2x), and Visa Signature transactions that only earned the base 0.5% reward-used to size next month's ACH allowance.
    Push to Card
    Fold's instant conversion that sells bitcoin balances and loads the USD proceeds to your Fold Card, typically within 1-10 minutes.
    Fold+
    Fold's paid subscription that unlocks 0% bitcoin buy/sell fees, 0.5% base card rewards, rotating Boosts, ACH Bill Pay rewards, and higher instant transfer limits.

    Privacy & Safety

    Who can see what when you use Fold

    • KYC information you submit is checked against public records and may be reviewed manually for 3-4 business days when verification fails.
    • USD card funds are held at Sutton Bank, so the bank and Fold have visibility into your account activity for compliance and FDIC pass-through coverage.
    • Bitcoin you hold in Fold remains with custodial partners such as BitGo until you withdraw it to self-custody.
    • Fold may rotate deposit addresses after each confirmed receive; reusing older addresses can reduce your privacy.
    Do: Plan to withdraw or sweep bitcoin balances you intend to custody yourself once they settle.
    Don't: Assume Fold provides on-chain privacy-address reuse and custodial storage link activity to your account.

    Lost your phone? How Fold recovery works

    • To move funds off Fold, go to the Bitcoin tab, tap Send -> Withdraw, enter your destination, and confirm the network fee.
    • Ensure your available balance exceeds the displayed miner fee before submitting a withdrawal so it can broadcast successfully.
    • Contact support@foldapp.com if a withdrawal or Push to Card conversion has not arrived after 24 hours.
    Do: Test a small withdrawal first and keep your destination wallet backups secure before moving larger amounts.
    Don't: Ignore app notifications-pending transactions still require network confirmations before the balance is spendable.

    Availability & safe installs

    • The Fold Prepaid Debit Card currently serves U.S. residents only.
    • Qualified spend and ACH reward calculations use UTC windows, so late-night transactions near month end may land in the next cycle.
    • You must maintain Fold+ membership for the entire month to earn ACH rewards; lapses reset eligibility until the following month.

    Quick answers

    Does the Fold Card work internationally?

    Yes, international transactions are supported, but standard network fees or issuer limits may apply and USD balances still live at Sutton Bank.

    How quickly will Push to Card funds show up?

    Push to Card usually loads USD within 1-10 minutes, though Fold notes rare delays of up to 24 hours.

    What should I do if identity verification fails?

    Double-check your personal details, avoid PO boxes, and allow 3-4 business days for manual review before retrying or contacting support.

    Key terms
    Qualified spend
    The tally of eligible Fold activity in the prior month that sets your ACH Bill Pay reward allowance.
    Instant withdrawal limit
    Fold's cap on instant debit transfers (e.g., $250 per withdrawal for long-tenured Fold+ members) before ACH settlement clears.
    BitGo insurance
    Coverage maintained by Fold's custodial partner BitGo against theft of private keys while your bitcoin stays in Fold.

    Fold aims to make Bitcoin accessible by integrating it into daily financial activities. It offers a Visa prepaid debit card, gift card purchases, and bill payments, all rewarding users with Bitcoin (sats). Users can also buy Bitcoin with low or no fees, depending on their plan, and deposit Bitcoin into their Fold account for insured custody.

    What sets Fold apart is how it combines traditional finance functions like bill pay and debit card usage with Bitcoin rewards in one simple interface. Users don't need to juggle multiple apps or wallets. Rewards can be earned on nearly every transaction, from daily spending to mortgage payments, with bonus sats for gift card purchases. Fold also allows external Bitcoin deposits and provides low-fee conversions from paycheck to BTC, making it easy to live on a Bitcoin standard while minimizing taxable events.

    Users praise the ease of use, the ability to stack Bitcoin passively, and the clear design of the app. Some power users run most of their household finances through Fold, using the gift card section for groceries and shopping, and setting up recurring buys with leftover balances at the end of each month. The ability to earn rewards even on ACH bill pay is seen as a unique benefit.

    Fold's vision is to be the ultimate platform for living on a Bitcoin standard, making it easy to earn, spend, and save Bitcoin while navigating a fiat world, giving users control and flexibility without the complexity of traditional exchanges or wallets.

    FAQs

    How do Fold rewards differ by plan?

    Fold's Basic plan lets you spin the Rewards Wheel, buy gift cards, and refer friends for sats, while Fold+ subscribers add guaranteed 0.5% back on signature Visa purchases, 1.5%-5% rotating Card and Merchant Boosts, and ACH Bill Pay rewards.

    How do ACH Bill Pay rewards work?

    Fold+ members earn 0.25%-1.5% back on ACH Bill Payments up to the amount of the previous month's qualified spend-the first $2,500 in payments uses your tier rate, dollars $2,501-$5,000 revert to 0.25%, and anything beyond last month's spend earns 0%. Qualified spend includes bitcoin buys, gift cards purchased with your Fold balance (counted 2x), and Visa Signature transactions that only received the base 0.5% reward.

    What limits apply to the Fold Card?

    As of September 5, 2024 the Fold Card caps include a $30,000 account balance, $15,000 per-day spend, $15,000 per calendar-day loads, a $2,000 rolling 24-hour gift card limit, and $250 per ATM transaction ($1,000 per day/month). Instant debit transfers cap at $1,500 per deposit and depend on how long you have held Fold+.

    Is the Fold Card FDIC insured?

    USD on the Fold Card is issued by Sutton Bank, Member FDIC, so balances qualify for pass-through FDIC insurance up to $250,000 per ownership category when eligibility conditions are met. Bitcoin balances, rewards, and gift card value are not FDIC or SIPC protected.

    How long does Push to Card take and what does it cost?

    Push to Card sells bitcoin inside Fold and loads USD to your Fold Card; Fold+ members pay no fee, Basic users pay a 1% sell fee, and transfers usually settle in 1-10 minutes (rarely up to 24 hours).

    How do I withdraw bitcoin from Fold?

    Open the Bitcoin tab, tap Send -> Withdraw, paste or scan your destination address, and confirm; the only cost is the Bitcoin network miner fee. Most withdrawals finalize within 1-2 hours, and Fold asks you to contact support if a transfer hasn't landed after 24 hours.
