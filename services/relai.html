    Quick verdict

    Best for: European savers who want bitcoin delivered straight into a self-custodial app with minimal steps.

    Consider if: You plan to pair a hardware wallet with recurring buys and prefer SEPA funding over keeping cash on an exchange.

    Not ideal when: You need Lightning withdrawals, desktop trading tools, or live outside Relai's supported countries.

    Getting started

    Getting started

    1. Create & verify

    Time: ~10 minCost: FreeRisk: Low
    1. Install Relai from the App Store or Google Play and create your wallet.
    2. Write your 12-word recovery phrase on paper, confirm it in-app, and store it safely offline.
    3. Complete the ID verification Relai requests so you can fund larger orders without delay.

    Gotcha: Never take a photo of the recovery phrase or store it in email.

    2. Fund (SEPA / card)

    Time: ~5 min (card) / ~1-2 biz days (SEPA)Cost: VariesRisk: Low
    1. Tap the + button, choose Buy, then add your SEPA bank account or a card for instant funding.
    2. If you pick SEPA, copy the Relai IBAN exactly and initiate the transfer from the same-name bank account.
    3. For cards, complete the 3-D Secure approval and confirm you accept the ~3% processor fee.

    Gotcha: SEPA transfers must match the currency quoted in the order or your bank may reverse the payment.

    3. Buy & withdraw

    Time: Instant with cards / ~10–30 min after SEPA postsCost: 1% fee (0.9% with invite) + spreadRisk: Medium
    1. Set a one-time amount or schedule an auto-invest plan and review the fee summary.
    2. Leave delivery on the in-app wallet or toggle external wallet to paste a hardware wallet address.
    3. Confirm the order; Relai covers the mining fee and broadcasts the transaction once the payment clears.

    Gotcha: Auto-invest stays free only on your first active plan up to 100 CHF/€ each month.

    Quick answers

    How do I connect a hardware wallet to Relai?

    Open the Relai web app, go to Settings > Wallets, choose Add new wallet address, select your hardware wallet, and sign the message on the device.

    How long do SEPA transfers take to fund a Relai order?

    Most SEPA transfers arrive within one to two business days; Relai executes the order and broadcasts the bitcoin once funds land.

    Can I edit or pause a Relai auto-invest plan?

    Yes. Open Orders, tap your plan, and you can change the amount, pause the schedule, or cancel it entirely.

    Quick answers

    Does Relai cover the bitcoin network fee when I buy?

    Yes. Relai pays the mining fee on purchases, so the sats you receive already include the on-chain cost.

    How does the 0% auto-invest tier work on Relai?

    Your first active auto-invest plan up to 100 CHF/€ per month runs at 0%; any extra volume or additional plans revert to the standard 1% fee.

    How can I reduce Relai fees?

    Add an invite code to drop buys to 0.9%, stick with SEPA transfers to avoid card fees, and schedule buys within the free auto-invest allowance.

    Self-custody: withdraw safely

    Self-custody: withdraw safely

    1. Pick a cold storage wallet

    Time: ~5 minCost: €0–€200Risk: Low

    Cold storage keeps long-term savings offline and away from phone risks.

    Show steps
    1. Choose a cold storage wallet that fits your budget and skill level.
    2. Initialize the hardware wallet or cold storage software and generate a new seed phrase.
    3. Record the seed on paper or metal and store backups in separate secure locations.

    Gotcha: Never store the new seed digitally or reuse the seed Relai generated for your mobile wallet.

    2. Create a fresh receive address

    Time: ~2 minCost: FreeRisk: Low

    Fresh addresses protect privacy and make tracking easier.

    Show steps
    1. Connect your cold storage wallet to its desktop or mobile companion app.
    2. Generate a new receive address and verify it on the hardware wallet screen.
    3. Copy the address to Relai when you are ready to move larger balances.

    Gotcha: Use each address once; hardware wallets make it easy to generate a new one for every withdrawal.

    3. Send a small test amount

    Time: ~5 minCost: Mining fee (~€1–€5)Risk: Low

    Testing the flow confirms your address and keeps stress low for bigger moves.

    Show steps
    1. Open Relai, tap Send, and paste the fresh address from your cold storage wallet.
    2. Send a small amount (~€20) using the network fee Relai quotes.
    3. Confirm the transaction in your hardware wallet app or on a block explorer.

    Gotcha: Wait for at least one confirmation before moving the rest.

    4. Withdraw the remaining balance

    Time: ~10–60 minCost: Mining fee (~€5–€20)Risk: Low

    Finish the migration so your long-term sats live in cold storage.

    Show steps
    1. Generate another fresh address for the main withdrawal.
    2. Send the remaining balance from Relai and record the transaction ID with your backups.
    3. Export Relai transaction history so you can track cost basis later.

    Gotcha: Relai covers mining fees on buys but not on withdrawals, so wait for quieter mempool periods when possible.

    Quick answers

    Can Relai recover my wallet if I lose the seed phrase?

    No. Relai never sees your 12-word seed, so the only recovery path is the backup you create.

    Does Relai support multisig wallets?

    Relai does not coordinate multisig today; pair the app with a hardware wallet or move large balances to a dedicated multisig provider.

    How often should I rotate receiving addresses in Relai?

    Relai issues a fresh address for every order; keep using new addresses to limit on-chain linking.

    Privacy & Safety

    Privacy & Safety

    Who can see what when you use Relai

    • Relai retains your name, address, and ID scans once you complete verification.
    • Card and mobile payments route through payment processors that can view your card details and approval history.
    • Support agents can access order history and delivery addresses to resolve funding or payout issues.
    • Your 12-word seed lives only on your device; Relai does not upload or store it on their servers.
    Do: Lock the Relai app with a device passcode, review connected devices regularly, and revoke access you no longer use.
    Don't: Share screenshots of your orders or seed phrase in chat apps or email threads.

    Lost your phone? How Relai recovery works

    • Reinstall Relai on a new phone and choose Restore wallet when prompted.
    • Enter your 12-word seed carefully and keep the app open until balances resync.
    • Contact support only if you still see zero balance after the blockchain sync completes.
    Do: Store the seed offline, test a restore when convenient, and protect your email with its own two-factor authentication.
    Don't: Delete the app or wipe your phone before you confirm the restore worked.

    Availability & safe installs

    Relai, founded in 2020 in Zürich, Switzerland, is an app designed for beginners to buy and save Bitcoin easily. It stands out from competitors with its non-custodial wallet, meaning users control their Bitcoin via a 12-word seed phrase. After purchasing Bitcoin, it will be directly sent to your wallet.

    Relai offers purchases starting at €50 | 54,205 sats, one free buy up to €100 | 108,409 sats monthly, until you hit €1200. After that you pay a 1% fee for any transaction. For businesses and wealthy individuals Relai offers personal service and expert guidance.

    Its app, rated 4.5 stars on iOS, is praised by users for simple onboarding and smooth navigation, though some users report verification delays or high card fees. Relai focuses on privacy and self-custody, with optional KYC for small transactions.

    Its vision is to make Bitcoin saving accessible across Europe, with plans to add Lightning Network support for faster payments. Beginners love its straightforward design and security, but it's limited to Europe and lacks advanced savings features.

    FAQs

    How do Relai's bitcoin buying fees work?

    Relai charges a 1% service fee on each buy, drops to 0.9% when you enter an invite code, applies a dynamic spread of up to 1%, and waives the fee on your first auto-invest plan up to 100 CHF/€ per month.

    Does Relai let me buy bitcoin straight to my hardware wallet?

    Yes. Open Settings > Wallets in the Relai web app, connect your BitBox, Ledger, or Trezor, and sign the verification message so future buys settle to that address.

    What is the minimum order size on Relai?

    Relai orders start at 50 CHF or €50 whether you buy once or set up an auto-invest plan.

    What payment methods does Relai support for buying bitcoin?

    You can fund purchases with a manual SEPA transfer or use credit and debit cards, Apple Pay, or Google Pay for instant settlement, though cards add about 3% per order.

    Where is Relai available today?

    Relai focuses on Switzerland and the broader EEA, so confirm availability in your local app store and review which payment options are enabled in your country.
