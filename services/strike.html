    Quick verdict

    Best for: Buyers who want to DCA and withdraw over Lightning without extra fees.

    Consider if: You need bank or debit funding with predictable limits and recurring buys.

    Not ideal when: You require support for other cryptocurrencies or desktop trading.

    Getting started

    1. Create & verify

    Time: ~10 minCost: FreeRisk: Low
    1. Install Strike from App Store or Google Play and sign up with your email
    2. Submit your government ID photo and provide your legal name, address, and SSN
    3. Wait for verification to complete—contact support if it takes longer than 24 hours

    Gotcha: Limits stay conservative until verification clears.

    2. Fund (ACH / card / SEPA)

    Time: ~1 min (card) / ~1 day (wire) / ~5 biz days (ACH)Cost: VariesRisk: Low
    1. Open Account → Payment methods and link your bank via Plaid or add a debit card
    2. Review your deposit limits for ACH, card, or wire before funding
    3. Deposit cash—ACH trades instantly but stays pending ~5 business days before withdrawal

    Gotcha: ACH sits in pending ~5 biz days before you can withdraw.

    3. Buy & withdraw

    Time: ~1–30 minCost: 0.99%→0.39% + mining (on-chain)Risk: Low
    1. Tap Buy Bitcoin, enter an amount, or set up a recurring purchase to dollar-cost average
    2. Test withdrawal: tap Bitcoin → Send, scan a Lightning invoice or paste a Bitcoin address, and send a small amount (~$5–$10)
    3. Once verified, send the rest—Lightning is instant and free; on-chain offers Priority (~10 min), Standard (~1 hour), or Flexible (~24 hours, free)

    Gotcha: Flexible on-chain is free but ~24h; Standard ~1h.

    Quick answers

    How long does Strike verification take?

    Strike verification typically completes within a few minutes, though it can take up to 24 hours during high volume. If your account isn't approved within 24 hours, contact Strike support with your sign-up email.

    Can I buy bitcoin on Strike without verification?

    No. Strike requires full identity verification before you can deposit funds or buy bitcoin. You must submit a government-issued ID and complete KYC to meet regulatory requirements.

    What's the fastest way to buy bitcoin on Strike?

    Link a debit card for instant deposits and immediate bitcoin purchases. Card funds are available to withdraw right away, while ACH deposits trade instantly but stay pending about five business days before you can send bitcoin off Strike.

    Quick answers

    Does Strike charge network fees on Lightning withdrawals?

    Strike covers the Lightning routing cost, so you only pay the invoice amount. On-chain withdrawals still depend on the mining fee tier you pick.

    When does my Strike fee tier reset?

    Strike resets monthly trading volume at 00:00 UTC on the first day of the new month, placing you back on the highest fee tier until you trade again.

    Can I change the speed of an on-chain withdrawal after I send it?

    You cannot edit a broadcast once Strike submits it, but you can pick Priority, Standard, or Flexible before confirming, and auto-withdrawals give you a 12 hour cancel window.

    Privacy & Safety

    Who can see what when you use Strike

    • Strike retains your legal name, address, phone number, and SSN or national ID to verify the account.
    • Connected banks and cards route through Plaid and card processors that can see your account and routing numbers.
    • Strike records Lightning invoices, on-chain addresses, and order history for compliance and customer support.
    • Support escalations may require you to confirm identity details again before Strike re-enables withdrawals.
    Do: Turn on two-factor authentication and review recent login sessions in Account > Security.
    Don't: Reuse the same password you use on other services or share your ID documents outside Strike's secure upload flow.

    Lost your phone? How Strike recovery works

    • Use the email verification link or code Strike sends to reset your password.
    • If 2FA is enabled, enter your authenticator code or backup code to finish the reset.
    • Contact support with your legal name and ID if you no longer control the registered email.
    Do: Store authenticator backup codes offline and keep your email secured with its own 2FA.
    Don't: Wait until your account is fully locked before you reach out to Strike support.

    Availability & safe installs

    • Strike operates in the United States (except New York) and 95+ other countries.
    • Recurring purchases, Bill Pay, and Strike Instant availability depends on your region.
    • Confirm local limits and payment methods in Account > Limits before funding.

    Does Strike support hardware wallet withdrawals?

    Yes. You can paste any Bitcoin address from your hardware wallet or trigger an auto-withdrawal to that address.

    Can I change my Lightning withdrawal address after triggering auto-withdrawal?

    You can edit your address list before the auto-withdrawal broadcasts. Each run gives you a 12 hour cancellation window.

    How can I track past withdrawals?

    Open Bitcoin > Activity to view completed auto-withdrawals, Lightning sends, and on-chain transactions.

    Strike features & limits

    Bill Pay

    Works, with caveat

    Live on bitcoin Auto-pay from paycheck

    Pay bills directly from your Strike balance while maintaining bitcoin exposure. Convert your paycheck to bitcoin via direct deposit, then let Strike handle rent, utilities, and credit cards automatically.

    Show steps

    Try it:

    1. Tap Cash > Bill Pay and generate your unique Strike account and routing numbers
    2. Set your default payment source to Cash or Bitcoin
    3. Add your Strike account details to your employer's direct deposit and to each biller's payment system
    4. Bills auto-pay from your chosen balance each month

    Gotcha: Paying bills from your bitcoin balance triggers a taxable event—Strike sells bitcoin to cover each payment. Custodial setup: you still hold counterparty risk while funds sit on Strike.

    Bitcoin-backed loans

    Works, with caveat

    9.5% APR Liquidation risk

    Access cash without selling your bitcoin. You retain ownership and future upside while Strike holds your bitcoin as collateral.

    Show steps

    Try it:

    1. Ensure your Strike account is fully verified and you're in a supported U.S. state
    2. Tap Cash > Bitcoin-backed loans
    3. Choose your loan amount and add the required bitcoin collateral
    4. Review the Loan Agreement (interest rate, liquidation threshold, repayment terms)
    5. Receive cash in your Strike account after approval

    Gotcha: If bitcoin's price drops below the liquidation threshold, Strike may sell your collateral to cover the loan. Loan availability and minimums vary by state. You give up self-custody while bitcoin sits as collateral.

    Auto-withdraw

    Works

    Free (Flexible tier) 12-hour cancel window

    Automatically move your bitcoin to self-custody once your balance reaches a threshold you set, reducing time on exchange.

    Show steps

    Try it:

    1. Open Bitcoin settings and tap Auto-withdraw
    2. Set your threshold between 1,000,000 and 500,000,000 sats
    3. Add one or more payout addresses (on-chain Bitcoin addresses)
    4. When your balance crosses the threshold, Strike queues the withdrawal with a 12-hour cancel window

    Gotcha: Auto-withdrawals use the free Flexible tier, which can take up to 24 hours to confirm. You can cancel during the 12-hour window before broadcast.

    3. Copy the Bitcoin address (starts with bc1, 1, or 3)
    4. Use this address only once for your Strike withdrawal

    Gotcha: Never reuse Bitcoin addresses. Hardware wallets generate fresh addresses automatically for privacy.

    3. Send $5–$10 worth of bitcoin using Standard tier (~1 hour)
    4. Wait for confirmation then verify in your hardware wallet

    Gotcha: Use Standard tier for reliable but cost-effective confirmation. Priority is faster but more expensive.

    4. Withdraw the remaining balance

    1. For smaller amounts: use Standard tier (~1 hour, ~$5–$10 fee)
    2. For larger amounts: use Flexible tier (~24 hours, free)
    3. Generate a fresh address for your remaining withdrawal
    4. Send the rest of your balance and store your hardware wallet securely

    Gotcha: Flexible tier is free but can take up to 24 hours. Standard tier balances speed and cost effectively.

    Quick answers

    Why should I use cold storage?

    Cold storage keeps your bitcoin offline, protecting it from hackers and online threats. Hardware wallets provide military-grade security for long-term holding.

    How do I verify my withdrawal arrived?

    Check your hardware wallet's transaction history or use a block explorer like mempool.space. On-chain transactions confirm within 10 minutes to 24 hours depending on your fee tier.

    What's the safest way to store my seed phrase?

    Memorize your 12-24 word seed phrase or write it on paper or metal. Store copies in separate secure locations like a fireproof safe and safety deposit box. Never store it digitally.

    FAQs

    How do Strike's bitcoin trading fees work?

    Strike charges a tiered Strike fee that starts at 0.99% for monthly volume under $250 and steps down to 0.39% once you trade more than $15,000,000. Recurring purchases skip the fee after the first cycle for hourly or daily schedules and after the second run for weekly or monthly schedules.

    When are ACH deposits available to withdraw?

    ACH deposits post instantly for trading but sit in your pending balance until the transfer settles, which typically takes about five business days plus any extra hold Strike applies based on your history. Once the deposit moves to available balance you can send, spend, or withdraw it.

    Are Lightning withdrawals free?

    Yes. Lightning withdrawals from your available bitcoin balance have no Strike fee and settle instantly once your ACH or card deposit has cleared.

    How do bitcoin auto-withdrawals work?

    You can set a threshold between 1,000,000 and 500,000,000 sats along with one or more payout addresses. When your available balance crosses the threshold Strike queues the full amount, gives you a 12 hour window to cancel, then broadcasts within 24 hours using the fee-free Flexible tier.

    Where is Strike available today?

    Strike is available across the United States except New York, Puerto Rico, and in more than 95 other countries. Availability and payment methods vary, so confirm support in your local App Store or on the Strike website.
