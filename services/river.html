    Quick verdict

    Best for: U.S. buyers who want high-touch custody plus bitcoin interest payouts.

    Consider if: You need concierge funding, River Link, and beneficiary planning under one login.

    Not ideal when: You want self-custody or service outside the United States.

    Getting started

    Getting started

    1. Create and verify your account

    Time: ~10 minCost: FreeRisk: Medium
    1. Sign up at river.com or install the River app from your app store.
    2. Enter your legal name, address, Social Security number, and upload a government ID when prompted.
    3. Turn on two-factor authentication inside Settings > Security before funding the account.

    Gotcha: River stays custodial until you withdraw, so plan your exit path before you fund it.

    2. Fund with ACH, wire, or direct deposit

    Time: ~1-5 business daysCost: VariesRisk: Medium
    1. Link your U.S. bank account via Plaid or copy your River routing and account numbers for payroll.
    2. Schedule an ACH transfer or initiate a wire; wires usually clear in about one business day while ACH settles in roughly a week.
    3. Watch the Funding tab; cash trades immediately but moves from pending to available once settlement finishes.

    Gotcha: ACH withdrawals unlock after funds settle, which typically takes around seven days.

    3. Buy bitcoin and set your exit

    Time: ~5 minCost: 0.99% -> 0.25% + network feesRisk: Medium
    1. Place an instant buy or create a zero-fee recurring order so future DCA runs without River's trading fee.
    2. Enable Auto Withdraw or queue manual withdrawals to your hardware wallet once funds become available.
    3. Test a Lightning send through River Link to confirm you can move sats off the platform on demand.

    Gotcha: Zero-fee recurring buys skip fees after the first seven days but the bitcoin still sits in River custody until you withdraw.

    Quick answers

    How long before I can withdraw an ACH deposit?

    ACH cash trades instantly but River and Lead Bank release it for withdrawals after settlement, usually in about seven days.

    Does all of my cash earn bitcoin interest?

    Once you enroll, every settled dollar in your cash balance accrues interest daily and River converts the payout to bitcoin each month.

    Can River handle business or trust accounts?

    Yes. River offers business, private client, and beneficiary accounts; contact support to open the right profile.

    Quick answers

    When can I withdraw an ACH deposit?

    Once River and Lead Bank mark your ACH transfer settled, usually about seven days after you initiate it, the funds move from pending to available and you can withdraw.

    Do Lightning withdrawals include fees?

    River covers Lightning routing so you only pay the invoice amount. On-chain withdrawals pass through the miner fee for the priority you choose.

    Can I opt out of bitcoin interest payouts?

    Yes. In Cash > Interest you can switch payouts to USD or unenroll entirely if you prefer not to receive bitcoin.

    Bitcoin Interest on Cash

    Works, with caveat

    3.5% APY Custodial exposure

    River pays 3.5% APY on idle cash and converts the interest to bitcoin each month. Your cash stays custodial during this time, which means trusting River's reserves without per-user on-chain proof. Some people use this for working capital while moving long-term holdings to self-custody; others prefer to avoid custodial yield arrangements entirely.

    Show steps

    Try it:

    1. In Cash, opt into Bitcoin Interest on Cash
    2. River accrues interest daily and converts to bitcoin monthly
    3. You can sweep earned sats to self-custody after each payout
    4. Different people draw different lines about how much to keep custodial versus self-custodied

    Gotcha: Interest payouts stay custodial until you withdraw. You can set Auto-withdraw to sweep sats automatically or manage this manually based on your preferences.

    Works

    Auto-withdraw

    Works

    Free (Flexible tier) Scheduled withdrawals

    When your balance hits a threshold you set, River automatically moves sats to your wallet using the free Flexible tier. This reduces the time your bitcoin sits in custody, though you give up some control over the exact timing and fee selection compared to manual withdrawals.

    Show steps

    Try it:

    1. Open Settings and tap Auto-withdraw
    2. Set your threshold amount in sats
    3. Add one or more payout addresses to your self-custody wallet
    4. River queues the withdrawal within 24 hours once you cross the threshold

    Gotcha: Auto-withdrawals use the Flexible tier, which takes about 24 hours to confirm. ACH-funded bitcoin must settle first before any withdrawal can process.

    Quick answers

    Why bother with self-custody if River is full reserve?

    Full reserve reduces rehypothecation risk but you still rely on River's controls and jurisdiction. Self-custody gives you final settlement without counterparty risk.

    Can I send to a Lightning wallet instead of on-chain?

    Yes. River supports Lightning withdrawals at no added fee, which is handy for topping up a mobile wallet before moving larger balances on-chain.

    What happens to auto-withdrawals if fees spike?

    Auto-withdrawals default to the flexible tier. Pause or cancel them during high fee spikes and send manually with a fee tier that matches your urgency.

    Privacy & Safety

    Privacy & Safety

    Who can see what when you use River

    • River holds your personal information, banking details, and transaction history to comply with U.S. financial regulations.
    • Proof of Reserves publishes anonymized liability chunks, so outsiders cannot see account-level balances but can verify totals.
    Do: Export statements regularly and delete unneeded banking links if you change providers.
    Don't: Assume Lightning activity is private. River and routing peers still see invoices and timestamp metadata.

    Lost your phone? How River recovery works

    • Reset your password from the login screen and confirm the email link River sends.
    • Provide your ID again if support escalates the recovery request.
    • Once access is restored, review ForceField settings and regenerate backup codes.
    Do: Store authenticator backup codes offline and keep your email secured with its own two-factor authentication.
    Don't: Reuse the same password you keep on other services or postpone recovery after a suspected compromise.

    Availability & safe installs

    • River focuses on U.S. clients; confirm availability before you attempt to open an account abroad.
    • Some states or business types may face additional verification or limits until River completes licensing.
    • Direct deposit and interest products may be unavailable outside eligible jurisdictions.

    Quick answers

    Does Proof of Reserves reveal my balance?

    No. River publishes aggregated liability data so you can verify totals without exposing individual accounts.

    Can I delete my personal data?

    You can request data deletion once regulatory retention periods end; contact support for the latest policy.

    What if I detect suspicious activity?

    Enable ForceField and contact River security immediately. They can freeze withdrawals while you update credentials.

    FAQs

    How do River's fees compare to staying on an exchange?

    River's trading fee starts at 1% and drops to 0.25% as your volume grows. Recurring buys skip fees entirely after the first week, and you can pair that with Auto-withdraw to move sats to self-custody automatically—though keeping some balance on River for active trading can make sense depending on your workflow.

    When can I withdraw ACH-funded bitcoin to my own wallet?

    ACH deposits trade instantly but stay pending about seven days until settlement clears. Once funds move to available balance you can withdraw to self-custody. River holds the keys during that time, which introduces the usual custodial and counterparty considerations.

    What's the exit path for moving sats off River?

    Lightning withdrawals are free and instant once your balance is available. On-chain offers Priority, Standard, and Flexible tiers depending on how quickly you need confirmation. Auto-withdraw can move sats automatically when you hit a threshold, though manual withdrawals give you more control over timing and fee selection.

    What's the trade-off with Bitcoin Interest on Cash?

    River pays 3.5% APY on idle cash and converts interest to bitcoin monthly. Your cash stays custodial during this time, which means trusting River's reserves without per-user on-chain proof. Some people use this for working capital while moving long-term holdings to self-custody; others prefer to avoid custodial yield entirely.

    Where is River available and what are the limits?

    River serves U.S. residents, though some states face additional verification steps until local licensing clears. The service is custodial, which means River holds the keys while you use the platform—you can withdraw to self-custody at any time once your deposits settle.
