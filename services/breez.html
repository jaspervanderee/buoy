    Breez (Misty Breez) focuses on making Lightning Network payments fast and easy. It stands out with its non-custodial mobile app, letting users control their Bitcoin, and a unique Point-of-Sale mode for merchants to accept instant payments. It offers sending, receiving, and storing Bitcoin via Lightning, plus a podcast player to support creators with Bitcoin tips.

    Breez (Misty Breez) focuses on making Lightning Network payments fast and easy. It stands out with its non-custodial mobile app, letting users control their Bitcoin, and a unique Point-of-Sale mode for merchants to accept instant payments. It offers sending, receiving, and storing Bitcoin via Lightning, plus a podcast player to support creators with Bitcoin tips.

    The app has a simple interface and no-setup payments, though some users report issues with cloud backups and wallet restoration.

    Breez's mission is to drive Bitcoin adoption for everyday use, with a vision to expand Lightning payments across apps using its Breez SDK, a free developer tool. The app currently suits everyday payments rather than very small transactions. Beginners love its ease and merchant tools, but some find technical issues frustrating.

    FAQs

    What is Breez Wallet?

    Breez is a non‑custodial Lightning wallet with PoS mode and a podcast player for streaming sats to creators.

    Does Breez require cloud backup?

    Yes. Breez uses a cloud backup to restore your wallet and Lightning channels, which some users prefer for convenience.

    Is Breez open source?

    Yes. Breez is open‑source and actively developed with the community.
