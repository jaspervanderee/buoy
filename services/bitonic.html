    Quick verdict

    Best for: Dutch and EU savers who want bitcoin-only buys with iDEAL and a fee-free monthly allowance.

    Consider if: You want bundled payouts that batch miner fees before moving sats to cold storage.

    Not ideal when: You need Lightning withdrawals or non-EU payment rails.

    Getting started

    Getting started

    1. Create and verify your account

    Time: ~10 minCost: FreeRisk: Low
    1. Visit Bitonic's Create account page and choose the personal or business flow.
    2. Accept the User Agreement and appendices, then submit your email and password.
    3. Upload the requested identity documents and confirm your IBAN so compliance can approve you.

    Gotcha: Verification can require additional documents, so keep your ID and proof of address nearby.

    2. Link your bank and schedule funding

    Time: ~5 minCost: Free-€0.15Risk: Low
    1. Add your EU bank account in the Bitonic app or dashboard.
    2. For Auto Invest, set up a recurring SEPA transfer from your bank to Bitonic's IBAN.
    3. If you prefer manual buys, prepare an iDEAL, Bancontact, or SEPA deposit to match your plan.

    Gotcha: Manual SEPA deposits take about one business day to clear before you can withdraw the resulting bitcoin.

    3. Buy and move sats to your wallet

    Time: ~5-30 minCost: Service fee + dynamic miner feeRisk: Low
    1. Choose Buy Bitcoin, enter your amount, or confirm your Auto Invest plan.
    2. Select 'My own wallet' to paste a fresh address or keep funds in Bitonic temporarily.
    3. Watch for the on-chain transaction and log it in your wallet once it confirms.

    Gotcha: Dynamic miner fees apply to every withdrawal, and urgent euro payouts cost 0.25% (minimum €7.50).

    Quick answers

    How long does Bitonic verification usually take?

    Most accounts are approved within minutes through the app, but manual review can extend the process if compliance needs extra documents.

    When will my Auto Invest transfer convert to bitcoin?

    Bitonic converts your standing SEPA payment as soon as the transfer arrives, then emails a receipt detailing the rate, fee allowance, and miner fee.

    Can I update my withdrawal address after scheduling a purchase?

    Yes. Edit the destination under Auto Invest or on the order form before the payout triggers so Bitonic pushes coins to the correct wallet.

    Quick answers

    Does Bitonic charge for iDEAL deposits?

    No. iDEAL deposits are free and post instantly so you only pay the trading service charge.

    How does Bitonic handle bitcoin withdrawal fees?

    Bitonic sets a dynamic miner fee that targets confirmation within three blocks and bumps it with Replace-By-Fee if confirmation lags.

    When should I request an urgent euro payout?

    Use the urgent payout when you need funds within the hour; it costs 0.25% with a €7.50 minimum and is available up to €10,000.

    Self-custody: withdraw safely

    Self-custody: withdraw safely

    1. Choose your cold storage

    Time: ~5 minCost: €0-€200Risk: Low

    Cold storage keeps bitcoin offline so exchange failures cannot touch it.

    Show steps
    1. Pick a hardware or multisig wallet that matches your experience.
    2. Initialize it offline and record the seed on paper or steel.
    3. Store backups in separate safe locations.

    Gotcha: Never photograph or email your seed phrase; offline copies are the only safe option.

    2. Add wallet address to Bitonic

    Time: ~2 minCost: FreeRisk: Low

    Linking your address turns every purchase into an automatic withdrawal.

    Show steps
    1. Generate a fresh receive address from your cold storage wallet.
    2. In Bitonic, choose "My own wallet" and paste the address carefully.
    3. Label the address in your wallet so you can confirm incoming funds later.

    Gotcha: Double-check the pasted address and consider a second device for verification to avoid clipboard malware.

    3. Send a test withdrawal

    Time: ~10 minCost: Dynamic miner feeRisk: Low

    A small test confirms your address and workflow before larger amounts.

    Show steps
    1. Buy a small amount of bitcoin or use part of your Auto Invest allowance.
    2. Trigger a withdrawal to your fresh address and note the transaction ID.
    3. Wait for one confirmation and verify the balance inside your wallet.

    Gotcha: Choose a standard fee tier so the test confirms within an hour without overpaying.

    4. Bundle and sweep the rest

    Time: ~10-60 minCost: Dynamic miner feeRisk: Low

    Bundled purchases reduce miner fees while automating self-custody exits.

    Show steps
    1. Enable bundled purchases in the Bitonic app and set your satoshi threshold.
    2. Confirm the destination address before the bundle triggers.
    3. After the sweep arrives, update your records and generate a new address for the next cycle.

    Gotcha: While funds are bundling they remain on Bitonic, so choose a threshold that balances fee savings with your appetite for custodial exposure.

    Quick answers

    Do I need to reuse addresses for Auto Invest?

    No. Bitonic lets you update the destination before each payout, so you can rotate addresses to protect privacy.

    How can I confirm my batched withdrawal arrived?

    Check your hardware wallet balance or look up the transaction ID on a block explorer once Bitonic emails the payout details.

    What if I want to pause withdrawals for a while?

    Disable bundled purchases or switch the destination back to your Bitonic account, then re-enable external payouts when you're ready to sweep again.

    Privacy & Safety

    Privacy & Safety

    Who can see what when you use Bitonic

    • Bitonic stores all account and transaction data on self-managed servers located in the Netherlands.
    • Identity documents, IBAN details, and bitcoin addresses are collected to meet Dutch anti-money laundering requirements.
    • Matomo analytics runs on Bitonic infrastructure without third-party cookies, so usage data stays in-house.
    • Payment partners such as Mollie, Volt, Worldline, Mailgun, and MessageBird receive only the data needed to process transfers and communications.
    Do: Request a data export via privacy@bitonic.nl if you want a copy or correction of your records.
    Don't: Reuse the same password or send KYC documents outside Bitonic's encrypted upload flow.

    Lost your phone? How Bitonic recovery works

    • Use the password reset link on the login form to regain access to your account email.
    • Follow any additional verification prompts Bitonic requires before trading resumes.
    • Contact support with your legal documents if automated recovery fails or you change your primary email.
    Do: Store updated contact details and keep two-factor authentication backups offline.
    Don't: Wait until you need an urgent withdrawal before testing your recovery options.

    Bitonic is the oldest and largest Bitcoin-only broker in the Netherlands, founded in 2012, with a reputation for reliability and simplicity. They offer fast, automatic Bitcoin delivery via iDEAL, SEPA, and Bancontact, and their fully automated system ensures quick transfers.

    Bitonic is the oldest and largest Bitcoin-only broker in the Netherlands, founded in 2012, with a reputation for reliability and simplicity. They offer fast, automatic Bitcoin delivery via iDEAL, SEPA, and Bancontact, and their fully automated system ensures quick transfers.

    It stands out with its direct purchase model, selling Bitcoin from its own stock for instant delivery and transparent pricing, avoiding third-party intermediaries. Bitonic offers buying, selling, and saving Bitcoin via iDEAL, SEPA, or Bancontact and provide an Auto Invest savings feature, allowing users to easily set up recurring Bitcoin purchases, (though only up to €250 | 271,023 sats) and a business solution for Bitcoin payments.

    Its app, rated 4.5 stars on iOS, is praised for its clean, no-nonsense design, though some users report slow verification. The platform uses multi-signature cold storage for security and encourages self-custody with personal wallets.

    Their vision focuses on making Bitcoin accessible and easy for Dutch and European users, backed by education through Bitonic Academy and news via Bitcoin.nl. Overall, Bitonic is ideal for beginners who want a reliable, no-altcoin platform in Europe, with fast transactions, clear pricing and a strong local support.

    FAQs

    How do Bitonic's Auto Invest fees work?

    Bitonic waives the service charge on the first €250 you auto-invest each calendar month, then charges 0.89% on additional volume plus a €0.15 SEPA processing fee and the prevailing network fee.

    Does Bitonic send bitcoin directly to my wallet after each purchase?

    Yes. You can link a personal bitcoin address so every buy settles to your wallet or use bundled purchases to queue a larger payout that reduces miner fees.

    What payment methods can I use to buy bitcoin on Bitonic?

    Bitonic supports iDEAL and manual SEPA deposits for free, Bancontact and Easy SEPA at 0.5%, and bitcoin deposits without any surcharge.

    How fast does Bitonic pay out when I sell bitcoin?

    Sales are reserved immediately and Bitonic submits the euro payout no later than the next business day, with an optional urgent transfer that costs 0.25% (minimum €7.50) for faster settlement.

    What verification does Bitonic require to open an account?

    You choose a personal or business flow, accept the User Agreement, and provide identity documents, address details, and IBAN information so Bitonic can meet Dutch compliance rules.
