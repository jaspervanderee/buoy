    Quick verdict

    Best for: Mobile Bitcoiners who want Liquid privacy and the option to spend over Lightning without running channels.

    Consider if: You plan to hold USDt on Liquid or need the Dolphin Card for day-to-day expenses.

    Not ideal when: You need rock-bottom Lightning fees or rely on desktop wallets.

    Getting started

    1. Install and create your wallet

    Time: ~5 minCost: FreeRisk: Low
    1. Download Aqua from the App Store or Google Play
    2. Create a new wallet and write the 12-word seed on paper
    3. Add an optional passphrase if you want an extra layer

    Gotcha: Aqua cannot import seeds from other wallets, so keep this new phrase safe.

    2. Add funds

    Time: ~10 minCost: Mining feeRisk: Medium
    1. Choose Receive and pick Bitcoin, Liquid, or Lightning
    2. For large on-chain deposits, use Direct Peg-in to land funds directly as L-BTC
    3. Wait for confirmations before spending

    Gotcha: Lightning receives run through Boltz, so expect a short delay and a swap fee when capacity is low.

    3. Pay or swap

    Time: ~1-3 minCost: 0.1%+ for Lightning swapsRisk: Medium
    1. Tap Send and pick Lightning, Bitcoin, or USDt
    2. Review the quoted fee before confirming
    3. Use the swap screen to bridge USDt to other chains if required

    Gotcha: Boltz and third-party providers charge extra for swaps; wait for low-fee periods if you're cost sensitive.

    Quick answers

    Why are Lightning fees higher than in other wallets?

    Aqua relies on Boltz swaps instead of native channels, so the service charges percentage-based fees on top of the Liquid mining cost.

    Can I choose which swap provider Aqua uses?

    Not yet. The app automatically selects a partner today, but JAN3 plans to let you pick providers in a future release.

    How do I keep swap fees predictable?

    Check the quoted fee before confirming and prefer Liquid or on-chain payments when you do not need an instant Lightning settlement.

    Key terms
    Boltz swap
    A trust-minimized service that turns your Liquid funds into Lightning payments and back.
    Liquid Network
    A Bitcoin sidechain that adds faster settlement, confidential transactions, and native USDt support.
    Direct peg-in
    An Aqua feature that converts on-chain BTC straight into your L-BTC balance without extra manual steps.

    Privacy & Safety

    Who can see what when you use Aqua

    • Aqua does not log payments on JAN3 servers, but third-party swaps (Boltz, SideShift, Changelly) see the amounts they process.
    • Optional JAN3 accounts (for services like Dolphin) store your email only; no password is kept.
    • App store billing and crash reports still follow Apple and Google policies.
    Do: Audit swap quotes and only share support details with providers you trust.
    Don't: Assume swaps or Lightning transactions are invisible. Providers learn the amounts they route.

    Lost your phone? How Aqua recovery works

    • Seed lives solely on your device; JAN3 cannot recover it if you lose it.
    • To restore, install Aqua on a new phone, choose Restore Wallet, and enter the 12 words (plus passphrase if set).
    • If Boltz fails to settle, Aqua shows a Refund button so you can reclaim the funds.
    Do: Store the seed somewhere dry and offline; consider adding the optional passphrase if you share the device.
    Don't: Never screenshot or cloud-sync the seed.

    Availability & safe installs

    • Dolphin Card roll-out is phased; some regions cannot apply yet.
    • Swap partners may block certain countries based on compliance rules.
    • Install updates promptly. New builds often address security and fee issues.

    Quick answers

    Does Aqua collect analytics?

    No. The wallet ships without analytics. Only optional services gather the email you volunteer.

    Can JAN3 recover my wallet if I lose the seed?

    No. The seed stays local, so recovery is entirely your responsibility.

    What happens if a Lightning swap fails?

    Boltz automatically refunds your Liquid balance. If it stalls, use the Refund button or contact the provider with the swap ID.

    Move your Aqua wallet or funds

    Restore Aqua on a new phone

    Time: ~10 minCost: FreeRisk: Low

    Choose: When you lose or upgrade your device and need the same wallet.

    Show steps
    1. Install Aqua from the official app store
    2. Pick Restore Wallet and enter the 12-word seed (and passphrase if you set one)
    3. Let the app rescan balances before deleting the old install

    Tips: Keep the old phone offline until the new one finishes syncing to avoid confusion.

    Gotcha: You cannot run the same seed on two phones at once.

    Another wallet → Aqua

    Time: ~5–15 minCost: Network feeRisk: Medium

    Choose: When you want Liquid privacy or the Dolphin Card but currently use a different wallet.

    Show steps
    1. Create your Aqua wallet and back up the seed
    2. Send a small test payment from your existing wallet to Aqua
    3. Move the remainder once the test settles

    Tips: Use Liquid or on-chain BTC for the transfer; Lightning swaps from other wallets will still incur fees.

    Gotcha: You cannot import an old seed, so funds must physically move.

    Aqua → Long-term storage

    Time: ~1–6 hoursCost: Mining feeRisk: Low

    Choose: When you want to vault BTC or USDt elsewhere.

    Show steps
    1. Send on-chain BTC or Liquid to your cold storage address
    2. If moving BTC, consider peg-out timing when fees are low
    3. Wait for confirmations before deleting Aqua

    Tips: Liquid peg-outs rely on SideSwap liquidity; plan ahead for large amounts.

    Gotcha: Peg-outs can take hours if SideSwap's hot wallet is empty.

    Aqua → Another wallet

    Time: ~15–45 minCost: Swap or routing feesRisk: Medium

    Choose: When you need native Lightning or desktop support instead.

    Show steps
    1. Install the new wallet and complete its backup
    2. Send a small test from Aqua using the network your new wallet prefers
    3. Drain remaining balances once confirmed

    Tips: Use on-chain transfers for large amounts to avoid repeated Boltz fees.

    Gotcha: Deleting Aqua without moving funds loses access permanently.

    FAQs

    What networks does Aqua support?

    Aqua keeps one balance spanning Bitcoin on-chain, the Liquid sidechain, and Lightning. You can also swap USDt to other chains through partners when needed.

    Does Aqua require KYC?

    No. The wallet is self-custodial. You only share an email if you opt into JAN3 services such as the Dolphin Card.

    How do Lightning payments work in Aqua?

    Aqua sends and receives Lightning through Boltz submarine swaps. You pay in L-BTC, Boltz settles over Lightning, and any refunds return to your Liquid balance.

    Can I import an existing seed phrase into Aqua?

    Aqua only restores wallets that were originally created inside the app. It generates a 12-word seed that you should store offline as your only backup.

    How does Aqua handle USDt on other blockchains?

    USDt on Liquid is native. When you send or receive USDt on networks like Ethereum or Tron, Aqua routes the transaction through swap providers such as SideShift or Changelly and their fees apply.
