    About Buoy

    Our Mission

    At Buoy, we guide new Bitcoin users through the world of Bitcoin-only services. Bitcoin represents freedom, fairness, and resilience in a world where traditional money is being devalued and controlled.

    What We Do

    We compare wallets, exchanges, merchant tools, and more with clarity, transparency, and a focus on user choice, privacy, and sovereignty. Our goal is to help you find tools that align with Bitcoin's principles, like self-custody and decentralization, so you can escape the broken fiat system.

    Our Vision

    We envision a world where people reclaim their financial freedom — where savings aren’t eroded by inflation and where individuals truly control their money. Bitcoin isn't just an asset; it's a protocol bounded by energy that reprices the world, creating exponential abundance as technology advances. In this future, everyone benefits from falling prices and rising productivity, without the need for endless debt and manipulation.

    Why We Exist

    Bitcoin services can be overwhelming for beginners. We break it down and compare key elements like custody models (do you control your keys?), KYC requirements (do you have to share personal info?), Lightning support (for fast, cheap payments), and fees—so you don’t have to. Most people are trapped in a system based on theft through inflation. Buoy helps you opt out by choosing Bitcoin-only tools that promote honesty, privacy, and real value creation.

    What Sets Us Apart

    • We only cover Bitcoin — no altcoins, no distractions.
    • We highlight non-custodial, open-source, and KYC-free options.
    • We provide clear side-by-side comparisons for faster, better decisions.

    Our Principles: Objectivity and Transparency

    We remain fully independent, with no affiliations or sponsorships influencing our recommendations. Our comparisons are based on transparent, verifiable criteria like open-source code, privacy protections, and user sovereignty—core to Bitcoin's ethos of decentralization and self-reliance. All data is openly sourced, and we welcome community feedback to ensure accuracy.

    Our Commitment

    Whether you're a beginner or a seasoned Bitcoiner, Buoy is here to help. We’re building for the long term, driven by feedback and Bitcoin values.
