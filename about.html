    About Buoy

    Our Mission

    At Buoy, we guide new Bitcoin users through the world of Bitcoin-only services. Bitcoin represents freedom, fairness, and resilience in a world where traditional money is being devalued and controlled.

    What We Do

    We compare wallets, exchanges, merchant tools, and more with clarity, transparency, and a focus on user choice, privacy, and sovereignty.

    Our Vision

    We envision a world where people reclaim their financial freedom — where savings aren’t eroded by inflation and where individuals truly control their money.

    Why We Exist

    Bitcoin services can be overwhelming for beginners. We break it down and compare key elements like custody models, KYC, Lightning support, and fees — so you don’t have to.

    What Sets Us Apart

    • We only cover Bitcoin — no altcoins, no distractions.
    • We highlight non-custodial, open-source, and KYC-free options.
    • We provide clear side-by-side comparisons for faster, better decisions.

    Our Principles: Objectivity and Transparency

    We remain fully independent, with no affiliations or sponsorships influencing our recommendations. Our comparisons are based on transparent, verifiable criteria like open-source code, privacy protections, and user sovereignty—core to Bitcoin's ethos of decentralization and self-reliance. All data is openly sourced, and we welcome community feedback to ensure accuracy.

    Our Commitment

    Whether you're a beginner or a seasoned Bitcoiner, Buoy is here to help. We’re building for the long term, driven by feedback and Bitcoin values.
