    Is Peach available outside EU?

    Yes. Peach is available globally with exceptions to the US, China and some OFAC/EU restricted countries.

    Does Relai allow credit cards?

    Yes. Relai supports card payments and mobile wallets (Credit Card, Apple Pay, Google Pay) alongside SEPA.

    Which has higher limits?

    It depends. Peach limits vary by counterparty, region, and your reputation on the platform. Relai uses app-defined tiers with a noted minimum buy amount.
