    FAQs

    Does Sparrow support multisig and hardware wallets?

    Yes. Sparrow can create and manage multisig and works with hardware wallets, with deep coin control and your own node.

    Is Unchained self-custody or custodial?

    Collaborative custody. Unchained uses 2-of-3 multisig where you hold two keys and Unchained can co-sign for recovery.

    Can Sparrow connect to my own node?

    Yes. Sparrow connects to Bitcoin Knots or Core, or your own Electrum server (including over Tor).
