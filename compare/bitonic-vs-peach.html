    Bitonic is the oldest and largest Bitcoin-only broker in the Netherlands, founded in 2012, with a reputation for reliability and simplicity. They offer fast, automatic Bitcoin delivery via iDEAL, SEPA, and Bancontact, and their fully automated system ensures quick transfers.

    Bitonic is the oldest and largest Bitcoin-only broker in the Netherlands, founded in 2012, with a reputation for reliability and simplicity. They offer fast, automatic Bitcoin delivery via iDEAL, SEPA, and Bancontact, and their fully automated system ensures quick transfers.

    It stands out with its direct purchase model, selling Bitcoin from its own stock for instant delivery and transparent pricing, avoiding third-party intermediaries. Bitonic offers buying, selling, and saving Bitcoin via iDEAL, SEPA, or Bancontact and provide an Auto Invest savings feature, allowing users to easily set up recurring Bitcoin purchases, (though only up to €250 | 271,023 sats) and a business solution for Bitcoin payments.

    Its app, rated 4.5 stars on iOS, is praised for its clean, no-nonsense design, though some users report slow verification. The platform uses multi-signature cold storage for security and encourages self-custody with personal wallets.

    Their vision focuses on making Bitcoin accessible and easy for Dutch and European users, backed by education through Bitonic Academy and news via Bitcoin.nl. Overall, Bitonic is ideal for beginners who want a reliable, no-altcoin platform in Europe, with fast transactions, clear pricing and a strong local support.

    Peach Bitcoin is a mobile-only peer-to-peer platform for buying and selling Bitcoin. It stands out with its non-custodial model, using multi-signature escrow to secure trades without holding user funds, shifting trust to individual traders, and offering KYC-free (Know Your Customer) transactions up to 1,000 CHF daily, emphasizing privacy.

    Users must build trust through reviews and completed trades to unlock higher transaction limits. It supports over 100 payment methods, including cash and gift cards, with a 2% fee for buyers and free selling.

    Peach's mission is to make peer-to-peer Bitcoin trading the standard, promoting financial freedom, with plans to enhance Lightning Network support and expand globally, especially in Latin America and Africa. The vision is to offer a private, on‑the‑go way to trade Bitcoin without heavy identity requirements, making it attractive to beginners who want control and privacy without overcomplicating the process. Beginners love its privacy and ease, but some find the mobile-only platform limiting.

    FAQs

    Is Peach available outside Europe?

    Yes. Peach is available globally with exceptions to the US, China and some OFAC/EU restricted countries.

    Does Peach have trade limits?

    Yes. Peach is KYC-free for smaller amounts; limits and verification can apply based on region and payment method.

    Does Bitonic support recurring DCA?

    Yes. Bitonic’s Auto Invest supports recurring buys, with a limited free tier.
