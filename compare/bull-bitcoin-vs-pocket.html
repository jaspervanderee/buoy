    Bull Bitcoin is a Canadian Bitcoin exchange that stands out by being entirely non-custodial, meaning users must provide their own wallet and take full ownership of their Bitcoin. It allows users to buy Bitcoin directly to their own wallets, sell Bitcoin instantly without waiting for confirmations, and even pay bills, rent, and credit cards in Canada by converting Bitcoin to fiat without relying on traditional banks. This approach positions Bitcoin as a tool for everyday exchange, not just long-term saving.

    Bull Bitcoin is a Canadian Bitcoin exchange that stands out by being entirely non-custodial, meaning users must provide their own wallet and take full ownership of their Bitcoin. It allows users to buy Bitcoin directly to their own wallets, sell Bitcoin instantly without waiting for confirmations, and even pay bills, rent, and credit cards in Canada by converting Bitcoin to fiat without relying on traditional banks. This approach positions Bitcoin as a tool for everyday exchange, not just long-term saving.

    The platform (the mobile app is in beta) is self-funded, Bitcoin-only, and deeply ideological, rejecting external investors. Its team identifies as cypherpunks, building open-source tools and promoting financial sovereignty. Bull Bitcoin's mission is to create sovereign individuals through uncompromising adherence to Bitcoin principles, making it attractive to users who want to align their actions with their values.

    Beginners find the platform easy to use, with a clean interface and responsive support, while advanced users appreciate its strong privacy stance and no-compromise philosophy. It's ideal for Canadians and expanding internationally for those who want full control over their Bitcoin without depending on custodians or legacy finance systems.

    FAQs

    Is Pocket non-custodial with auto-withdraw to your wallet?

    Yes. Pocket is non-custodial and delivers recurring bank-transfer purchases directly to your address.

    Does Bull Bitcoin support bill payments?

    Yes. In Canada, Bull Bitcoin lets you pay bills (rent, credit cards, etc.) by converting BTC to fiat via its bill-pay feature.

    Which supports recurring buys?

    Both. Bull Bitcoin and Pocket support DCA/recurring purchases to self-custody.
